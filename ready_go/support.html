2. Common Questions

Q: Where is my trip data stored?

A: All trip details, packing lists, and personal information are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to any cloud server.

Q: How does the AI packing feature work?

A: ReadyGo offers two AI options. Apple Intelligence runs entirely on-device with no data leaving your device. Cloud AI lets you connect your own API key to services like OpenAI or DeepSeek — trip context (destination, dates, purpose) is sent to generate suggestions, but no personal identifiers are included.

Q: Why isn't weather showing for my trip destination?

A: Weather forecasts require an active internet connection and are a ReadyGo Pro feature. If you're a Pro user and weather still isn't loading, try checking your network connection or restarting the app.

Q: How do I add family members to my trips?

A: Go to Settings (top-left sidebar) → Family Info. Add family members with their relationship and details. Then when creating or editing a trip, you can select who is traveling with you.

Q: How do I restore my Pro purchase?

A: Open Settings → Subscription, or tap "Restore Purchases" on any paywall screen. Purchases are linked to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

Q: Can I use ReadyGo offline?

A: Yes! Most features work offline including viewing trips, managing packing lists, and checking items. Weather forecasts and location search require an internet connection.
