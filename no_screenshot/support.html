1. Getting Started

1.1 How to Use

  1. Sign In: Use Sign in with Apple to create your account
  2. Add Content: Select screenshots from Photo Library or take new photos with Camera
  3. OCR Recognition: Text is automatically extracted from images (processed locally)
  4. Enhance with LLM: Use credits to format, summarize, or organize your text
  5. Save & Search: Your knowledge is stored locally and searchable

1.2 First-Time Credit Bonus

New users receive free credits upon first sign-in. Use them to try the LLM text enhancement features!

2. Features Explained

2.1 OCR (Text Recognition)

  • Powered by Apple Vision Framework
  • Processed 100% locally on your device
  • Supports multiple languages (Chinese, English, etc.)
  • No internet connection required

2.2 LLM Text Enhancement

  • Requires credits and internet connection
  • Available features: Summarize, Format, Extract key points
  • Original OCR text is sent to cloud for processing
  • Images are never uploaded - only text

2.3 Semantic Search

  • Uses local embedding model (CoreML)
  • Find related knowledge by meaning, not just keywords
  • All search happens on your device

3. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes! Your knowledge data (images, OCR text, notes) is stored entirely on your device. We cannot access it. Only when you use LLM features, the relevant text is sent to cloud for processing.
Q: Do I need internet to use the app?
A: Basic features (OCR, knowledge storage, search) work offline. Only LLM text enhancement requires internet connection.
Q: How do credits work?
A: Credits are used for LLM features. Each call consumes a certain amount. You can purchase more via in-app purchase or get free credits through events.
Q: Can I export my data?
A: Your data is stored locally in SQLite. Currently, there's no built-in export feature. Contact us if you need data export assistance.
Q: What happens if I delete the app?
A: All local data will be deleted. Your account and credits balance are saved on our servers - you can recover them by signing in again on a new installation.
Q: Which languages are supported for OCR?
A: The app primarily supports Chinese (Simplified) and English. Apple Vision Framework can also auto-detect other languages.
Q: How do I get my Support Code?
A: Go to "User Center" in the app. Your Support Code is displayed there and can be used for customer support conversations.

4. Troubleshooting

4.1 OCR Not Working

  • Ensure Camera and Photo Library permissions are granted
  • Try with a clearer image
  • Check if the image contains readable text

4.2 LLM Features Unavailable

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure you have sufficient credits
  • Try signing out and signing in again

4.3 Search Not Finding Results

  • Try different keywords or phrases
  • Use semantic search instead of exact match
  • Ensure you have saved knowledge items
