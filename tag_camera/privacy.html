Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-22

Overview

TagCamera ("we", "us", "our") is an iOS app for capturing photos and organizing them with tags. This Policy explains what data we process and why.

Data We Process

On-Device Data

  • Photos you capture or import within the app (stored locally and, if you choose, in the iOS Photos library).
  • Tags you create and assign to photos.
  • App settings (for example, the save-to-Photos preference).

Device Permissions

  • Camera: to capture photos.
  • Photos: to save to or read from your iOS Photos library when you opt in.

No Accounts, No Tracking

  • We do not require an account or login.
  • We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or location data.
  • We do not send your photos or tags to our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Sharing only happens when you explicitly use iOS share sheets to send a photo to another app or service.

Data Retention and Control

  • All app data stays on your device unless you share it or back it up via iOS mechanisms (for example, iCloud backup).
  • You can delete photos or tags at any time from within the app.

Security

Data is stored using iOS-provided storage. Protect your device with a passcode or biometrics to safeguard local data.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us to request deletion.

Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
