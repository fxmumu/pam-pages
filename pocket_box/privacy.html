Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2025

Thank you for using PocketBox ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Processing

Local Processing: PocketBox is designed to process your media files (videos and images) locally on your device. We do not upload your photos or videos to any external servers for processing. All conversions, compressions, and edits happen directly on your iPhone or iPad.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. We do not track your location or usage habits.

  • Trial Status: We store a secure token in your device's Keychain to manage your trial period. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • App Settings: Your preferences (such as theme or default output formats) are stored locally using UserDefaults.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Apple's services for core functionalities:

  • Apple StoreKit: Used to process In-App Purchases. Apple handles all payment transactions securely; we do not have access to your credit card information.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at foxdmu@gmail.com.
