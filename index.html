Frequently Asked Questions

Does PicSeek upload my photos?

No. PicSeek is designed to be 100% offline. Your photos are analyzed locally on your device and never sent to any server.

What AI models do you use?

We use optimized versions of CLIP (Contrastive Language-Image Pre-Training) models that run efficiently on Apple's Neural Engine.

Is it free?

PicSeek is free to download with core features. We offer a Pro subscription for advanced features like unlimited cleaning and cloud sync.

Does it work on iPad?

Yes! PicSeek is a universal app optimized for both iPhone and iPad.
