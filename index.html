The Problem

Tired of Photo Chaos?

We've all been there. You have thousands of photos, but you can't use them effectively.

"I can't find that one photo..."

You want to share a specific moment with a friend, but scrolling through 20,000 photos is impossible. You give up before you find it.

The Solution

Natural Language Search. Just describe what you remember: "red car by the beach" or "birthday cake 2023". We find it instantly.

"My storage is full of duplicates..."

Burst shots, screenshots, and similar angles are clogging your phone. Manually selecting them to delete is tedious and time-consuming.

The Solution

Visual Search & Batch Cleaner. We group similar photos and bursts together. Review them in one place and keep only the best shots.

"Organizing takes forever..."

You want to group photos by theme (Pets, Food, Travel), but picking them out one by one from the main timeline is exhausting.

The Solution

Collect Similar Images. Pick one "seed" photo (e.g., your cat), and we'll collect similar photos in batches into the current album for your review.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does PicSeek upload my photos?

No. PicSeek analyzes photos locally. Internet is only used to download your photos from iCloud if they aren't on your device.

What AI models do you use?

We use optimized versions of CLIP (Contrastive Language-Image Pre-Training) models that run efficiently on Apple's Neural Engine.

Is it free?

PicSeek is free to download with core features. We offer a Pro subscription for advanced features like unlimited cleaning, sharing, and syncing app albums to the system library.

Does it work on iPad?

Yes! PicSeek is a universal app optimized for both iPhone and iPad.
